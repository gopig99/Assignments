

What do you mean by Amazon Lex?
Amazon Lex is an amazon service for building conversational interface/chatbots to any application using voice or text.

What is the purpose of Amazon Lex?
To build,test,deploy conversational bots/chatbots either voice or text.

What is the foundation of Alexa? 
Amazon Lex power the Alexa Virtual Assistant

What are the advantages of Amazon Lex?
Easily publish the chatbot on apllications, Scales automatically, Cost effective & easy to use

What is Amazon Lex's full name?
Amazon Lex is an AWS service for building conversational interfaces for applications using voice and text.
